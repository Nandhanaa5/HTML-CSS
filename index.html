PORSCHE

PorscheAG is a German automobile manufacturer known worldwide for
its high-performance sports cars, SUVs, and sedans. Founded by Ferdinand Porsche in 1931 and
headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany, Porsche has become synonymous with precision engineering,
innovation, and luxury. Its lineup ranges from iconic sports cars like the 911 to electric
vehicles like the Taycan, offering thrilling driving experiences combined with advanced technology.
The brand's commitment to motorsport heritage ensures that every Porsche embodies superior
performance both on the road and track.

911 Models

The Porsche 911 is the brand’s most iconic model, introduced in 1964,
and has evolved over the decades to become one of the finest sports
cars in the world. The 911 is known for its rear-engine design, excellent
handling, and a perfect blend of luxury and performance. It has a variety
of variants tailored to different driving needs, including:


911 Carrera- The entry-level model, available as a coupe or cabriolet, offering a balance of performance and luxury.

911 Carrera S / GTS- More powerful versions of the Carrera, with improved engine tuning and sportier handling.

911 Targa– Featuring a distinctive removable roof, combining open-air driving with the coupe’s structure.

911 Turbo / Turbo S– Renowned for its blistering speed and advanced technology, making it a benchmark in supercar performance.

911 GT3 / GT3 RS– Track-focused models with a naturally aspirated engine, lightweight construction, and aerodynamic enhancements.

911 Dakar– A new addition inspired by rally racing, designed for off-road adventures with a raised chassis and rugged tires.

The 911 has retained its signature silhouette across generations, while consistently incorporating state-of-the-art technology to stay relevant and desirable.

Porsche India

Porsche India is the official arm of Porsche AG, responsible for sales and services across
the Indian market. Established in 2012, Porsche India operates under Volkswagen Group Sales
India Pvt. Ltd. The brand offers a range of its premium models in India, including the 911,
Taycan, Cayenne, Macan, Panamera, and 718 series.
Porsche dealerships in major cities like Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, and Hyderabad ensure a premium
customer experience. The 911 series remains one of the most popular Porsche models in India due to its heritage,
performance, and brand appeal among car enthusiasts. With an increasing focus on electric mobility, Porsche India
has also introduced the Taycan EV, reflecting the brand’s commitment to sustainability.
